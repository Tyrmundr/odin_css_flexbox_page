How about checking out some Runes of the Old Norse if we are doing this Odin Project thingy? Now, there are quite a lot of neat runes and they are mostly easy to learn. The god, Tiwaz. More commonly known as Tyr.

Haglaz or Hagalaz. It means "hail".

Odal or the more well-known Othala. It means heritage among others.

Raido. Not to be confused with "radio". It means ride and/or journey.
